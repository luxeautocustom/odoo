Backend Stripe Payment

Technology has enhanced almost everything, many business cooperation as well as other various sectors have transformed themselves completely digital. But with the intervention of technology, it's important to provide variety in all the services to customers in order to keep them happy and sustain a healthy bond with them.

One of the services is digital payment mode, where your customer has numerous options for online payment.In Odoo standard modules online payment options are only available on websites for portal customers. Odoo Backend Stripe Payment is a collaboration with Stripe Payment , commonly used for Digital Payment , which allows Admin or Account users to pay for their invoices using Stripe payment acquirer from their backend.

Customers will also be able to pay for their invoices directly from the invoice report

Access Rights

Menu sequence - Setting -> Users   Go to user profile & allow user for 'View Online Payment Options'.  

Invoice Online Payment

Invoicing -> Setting -> Customer payments  

Configuration

Menu sequence - Invoice -> Configuration -> Payment Acquirers -> Stripe   On Invoice Payment option set 'Automatic pay invoice on acquirer confirmation' & set related Payment Journal.

Customer Invoice Payment

Once customer invoice proceeds to 'Open' stage then pay with Stripe option will be visible on that invoice.

Stripe Checkout Box

After clicking on the 'Stripe' button, customers will be redirected to the Stripe website.  Here customer and total invoice amount related information will be fetched by the system and displayed to the customer.

Invoice Payment

Once user will click on 'Pay' then new payment will be created & invoice will move to 'paid' stage automatically.

Stripe Payment

At Stripe website side related payment entries will be get generated with invoice reference number.

Invoice Report

It is also possible to pay online directly from generated invoice report.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this module allow the user to capture payment manually after charge?

No, this module does not provide this feature currently

Can we pay an invoice using a saved token from the backend?

No, this module does not provide this feature currently

Can we refund the paid invoice from the backend?

No, this module does not provide this feature currently.

Does this module allow the user to pay an invoice partially?

No, this module does not provide this feature currently.

Does this module allow the user to void payment after charge?

No, this module does not provide this feature currently.

Does This module provide a feature to make payment from invoice reports?

Yes, you can pay your posted invoice from the generated report.

Is invoice payment automatically reconciled after complete payment from stripe checkout.

Yes, an invoice will automatically reconcile after completing the payment from stripe.
